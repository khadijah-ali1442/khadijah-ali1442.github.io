Cube Animation

I made these animations as a way to have fun while learning how to apply animation techniques in 3D, though eventually I did use a little 2D animation as well for the cubes' faces. I mostly used squish and stretch, mainly in their jumps. Since I wanted to keep the animation video nonverbal, the cubes' main way of communication were jumps and expressions. I tried to use the height, speed, and takeoff time of the jumps to show what the cubes felt or were trying to communicate to the other one. I wanted to give them character as well by hinting character traits through their reactions to certain things. For example, I intended to show the blue cube as being scared of heights by showing how reluctant it initially was to climb the structure they saw.
