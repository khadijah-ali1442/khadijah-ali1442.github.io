Scattered Storylines

Some time before making this artwork, I was lost on what I should even make art about since I didn't have much ideas. Then I remembered hearing one of my art teachers talking about the wide variety of things art can be made of and thought of making an artwork based off of an experience or feeling I felt before. I had decided to use what I felt when I used to try make stories set their own universes since I used to want to be an author. However, it became really difficult to finish them since it was hard to make scenes transition to another and keeping the pacing fast enough or slow enough. But since not all of the ideas could fit in one universe, I put them in another. Sometimes I would even borrow ideas from stories I didn't think would "make it", and soon I ended up with a lot of unfinished stories.

I decided to make some art based on how I felt when I had so many different storylines and universes in my mind. I chose to do a collage since that seemed to be the perfect way to easily show the overlapping ideas between the stories. The characters and weapons are all from different universes I was trying to build, and the textboxs and arrows were put over them to represent my thought process as I tried to put the scenes in chronological order.
