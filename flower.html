Somewhere in the grass

I started making this piece of work with the intention of collecting different materials to learn new techniques and apply them. In the end, the main thing I collected were origami sheets of different colours. After looking at them, I thought making a flower out of them would work well because of the colours available. I drew a rose in my sketchbook and used tracing paper to make individual petals and leaves on the sheets which I would cut out and assemble together on the background. For the background, I used a printing technique with acryllic paint I recently learned called Gelli Printing. After seeing how it would look, I decided to add an outline of the flower in sharpie and pen to make the piece more coherent.
