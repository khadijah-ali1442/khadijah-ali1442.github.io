My Room From Another Perspective

This artwork was made with the intention of drawing my room from another perspective. I first thought of making it from a literal other perspective, so I sketched out my room in pencil from the perpective of a spider from the ceiling. Then I thought of using a less literal idea of perspective, which I decided to represent in colour. One of the ways I used it were to separate which bed and closet belonged to me and which ones belonged to me sister, since it shows how I see those things as mine or not. However, I decided not to go with that for the other furniture since we share them. I originally also only wanted to use pen and pencil crayons, but after testing with some markers, I decided using markers for solid colour would be better than using pencil crayons.
